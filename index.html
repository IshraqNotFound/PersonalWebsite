About me

I am Muhammad Ishraq Hasin, born on July 24, 2009, and currently studying in Class 10 at Bangladesh Air Force Shaheen College Kurmitola, Dhaka Cantonment, Bangladesh. I have a strong passion for subjects such as Mathematics, Higher Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and Information & Communication Technology (ICT), as I enjoy the critical thinking and conceptual understanding they require, especially in areas like programming and digital technologies. I excel in Mathematics and am confident in solving complex problems. I have participated in extracurricular activities, such as the ICONIC IT Club’s quiz competition, where I proudly secured 3rd place. My future goal is to pursue a career in aviation, aiming to become a pilot, with a particular interest in technology-based fields like aviation, engineering, and ICT. I am continuously working on developing my programming skills and improving my presentation abilities. My approach to academics involves consistent study, including extra classes at coaching centers, and effective time management by studying during late hours when necessary.
